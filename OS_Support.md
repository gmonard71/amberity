# Linux OS support

List of Linux distributions to be supported, as of March 1, 2026:
- Debian 12 and 13
- Fedora 42 and 43
- OpenSuse 15.6 and 16.0
- RockyLinux 8, 9 and 10
- Ubuntu 22.04, 24.04, 26.04
