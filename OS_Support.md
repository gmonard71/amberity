# Linux OS support

List of Linux distributions to be supported, as of January 1, 2023:
- CentOS 7
- Debian 10 and 11
- Fedora 36 and 37
- OpenSuse 15.3 and 15.4
- RockyLinux 8 and 9
- Ubuntu 18.04, 20.04, 22.04, 22.10
